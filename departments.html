Services

Complete Blood Sugar Test (FBS & HbA1C)

It is crucial for identifying and managing diabetes, controlling blood sugar, and preventing complications.

Complete Blood Count (CBC), ESR & Urine Routine Test

Provides a snapshot of overall health, and identifies infections, anemia, inflammation, or kidney/urinary issues.

Kidney and Liver Function Tests (KFT & LFT)

Detects early signs of kidney or liver diseases, ensuring proper organ function.

Thyroid Function Profile

Monitors hormone levels to diagnose thyroid imbalances that affect metabolism, mood, and energy levels.

Vitamin Levels (Vitamin B12 & D)

Identifies deficiencies that impact energy, bone health, and nerve function.

Lipid Profile

Assesses cholesterol levels to evaluate heart health and detect risks of cardiovascular conditions.

Drugs of Abuse Panel

Tests for the presence of substances commonly abused, helping in diagnosis and monitoring addiction or misuse.

Hepatitis Panel

Screens for hepatitis viruses (A, B, C, etc.) to diagnose liver infections and prevent complications.

Hematology Tests (CBC, ESR, Coagulation)

Analyzes blood for anemia, clotting issues, infections, or other hematological disorders.

Tumor Markers

Identifies specific proteins or substances linked to certain types of cancers, aiding early detection and monitoring.

Immunology Tests

Evaluates immune system function, detecting autoimmune disorders, immune deficiencies, or infections.

Allergy Panel

Identifies specific allergens causing symptoms, helping in personalized allergy management and treatment.

Stool Routine Examination

Detects gastrointestinal infections, malabsorption, parasites, or bleeding in the digestive tract.

Ultrasound

Non-invasive imaging to visualize internal organs like the liver, kidneys, uterus, and more, for diagnosing various conditions.

X-Ray

A quick imaging test to assess bones, and lungs, or detect fractures, infections, and abnormalities.

Hormone Panel

Measures hormone levels (e.g., thyroid, estrogen, testosterone) to diagnose endocrine disorders or imbalances.

ECHO (Echocardiography)

Uses ultrasound to evaluate heart structure and function, identifying abnormalities or heart diseases.

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

Records the heart’s electrical activity to detect arrhythmias, heart attacks, or other cardiac issues.

Endoscopy

Visualizes the upper digestive tract to detect ulcers, growths, or other gastrointestinal issues.

Colonoscopy

Examines the large intestine to identify polyps, inflammation, or early signs of colorectal cancer.

Color Doppler Ultrasound

Evaluates blood flow in veins and arteries, helping diagnose blockages, clots, or circulatory issues.
